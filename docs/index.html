New Yorkers are crazy about films exclusively.

The 57th New York Film Festival (NYFF57) single ticketes were on sale on September 8th. The queue begins at noon EST. Everyone was placed into an online waiting room as long as he/she entered the NYFF website after 12 p.m. If you opened the website one hour later, unfortunately, you might find there were more than 4000 people ahead of you.

"All hipsters in NYC are ahead of me!" said Siyu Yang, a Chinese student in New York.

However, as the film audience grows bigger, film review industry still lacks enough diversity.

In 2018, USC Annenberg Inclusion Initiative published a report on gender/race of film critics, "Critic's Choice?: Gender and Race/Ethnicity of Film Reviewers Across 100 Top Films of 2017", finding that according to the reviews of 300 top films in 2015-2017, male critics account for 78.7%.
